## 4. A2P 10DLC COMPLIANCE AND REGISTRATION

**What is A2P 10DLC:**
A2P (Application-to-Person) 10DLC is a carrier requirement for all business SMS messaging in the US. Registration is mandatory and takes 3-7 business days.

**Client Responsibilities for Registration:**
Client must provide the following information within 3 business days of signing:
- Legal business name (as registered with IRS)
- Business EIN (Employer Identification Number) or Tax ID
- Business address
- Business type (LLC, Corporation, Sole Proprietor, etc.)
- Business website URL
- Description of how SMS will be used (provided by Service Provider)

**Registration Fees (Included in Setup):**
- One-time brand registration: $4.50-$46 (absorbed by Service Provider)
- One-time campaign registration: $15 (absorbed by Service Provider)
- Monthly campaign fee: $1.50-10/month (included in monthly fee)

**SMS Restrictions During Registration:**
- SMS functionality will not work until A2P registration is approved (3-7 business days)
- Voice calls will work immediately

**Compliance Requirements:**
- Client agrees their SMS use case is for customer care and lead follow-up only
- Client agrees not to use the system for cold outreach, marketing blasts, or spam
- Violation of compliance rules may result in number suspension by Twilio (not Service Provider's fault)
